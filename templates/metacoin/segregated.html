SEGREGATED ACCOUNTS



Fully Segregated Accounts for High Net Worth Individuals and Corporate Clients

Available to accounts of USD$1m and above, through our unique deals with leading financial institutions, your trading account can also be linked to a fully segregated private bank account.

You can trade directly on Meta Coin Options, without completing the actual transfer of your funds to us.

Your funds remain in your own name at one of our AA+ rated top tier banking partners, which are large globally recognized institutions, which have been individually chosen based on their extremely high ratings and solid credit-worthiness.

This means that you enjoy peace of mind that your funds are secure in your bank, whilst you are trading with Meta Coin Options.


With a Segregated Bank account option, institutional traders can benefit from:
  • Full segregation of individual client collateral according to strict financial
  • Regulatory requirements adhered to by the banking industry
  • Trade directly with Meta Coin Options without transferring funds
  • Easy and fast procedure for account opening
  • Full transparency and Weekly P/L setting

For clients with USD$1m or its equivalent in other currencies. How to Apply:
  1. Open trading account with Meta Coin Options, send required documentation and pass compliance checks
  2. Meta Coin Options will introduce client to the banking institution for bank account opening
  3. Provide all required documentation*
  4. Start Trading

*Full details on the required documentation is provided upon request, as this is subject to the circumstances of each application. Individual application and are subject to Meta Coin Options approval.
